## Overview

The Obolus Protocol consists of a suite of smart contracts on the **BNB Smart Chain (BSC) Testnet**. These contracts manage the minting of stablecoins, the trading of tokenized assets, and the confidential lending markets. 

All interaction intents are **sealed on-client** and only the **settlement outcomes** are visible on-chain, ensuring high privacy for institutional participants.

### How to use these addresses
- **Manual Verification:** You can search these addresses on the BNB Testnet Explorer to see the code and audit the protocol's holdings.
- **Wallet Integration:** Add the Token addresses (TSLAx, etc.) to MetaMask to see your balances once they are cleared by the protocol.
