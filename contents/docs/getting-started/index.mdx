## Introduction to Obolus

To begin interacting with the Obolus Network ecosystem, you'll need to set up your environment to communicate with the **BNB Smart Chain (BSC) Testnet**. 

Because Obolus is a **Server-Blind** protocol, your interactions (deposits, trades, loans) are handled differently than traditional DeFi. Your wallet doesn't just sign a transaction—it **seals an intent** that is later cleared by our decentralized settlement layer.

## Quick Start Path

1. **[Smart Contract Deployments](/docs/getting-started/deployments):** Get the core addresses and tokens for your wallet.
2. **[Architecture Deep Dive](/docs/architecture):** Learn how we protect your financial metadata through our 3-layer stack.

## Wallet & Network Setup

Add the BSC Testnet configuration to MetaMask or your preferred Web3 provider:

- **Network Name:** BSC Testnet
- **New RPC URL:** `https://data-seed-prebsc-1-s1.binance.org:8545`
- **Chain ID:** `97`
- **Currency Symbol:** `tBNB`
- **Block Explorer URL:** `https://testnet.bscscan.com`

## Faucets (Free Testnet BNB)

To pay for the gas of your settlement transactions, you can obtain test BNB from these fountains:
