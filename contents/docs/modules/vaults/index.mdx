## Institutional Grade RWA Vaults

Obolus Network's **Smart Vaults** are the primary entry point for users to deploy capital into yield-generating asset classes. These vaults bridge the gap between liquid on-chain positions and high-productivity off-chain RWAs.

### Key Vault Metrics

- **Total Value Locked (TVL):** The cumulative value of all assets deposited into the vault across all users.
- **Base APY:** The target annualized yield for the vault's underlying strategy.
- **Yield Boosting:** Additional rewards distributed to long-term stakers and governance participants.
- **Private Layer Participation:** Users can participate in any vault while their individual contribution remains cryptographically hidden.

### Supported Asset Classes

1.  **Equity-Linked Vaults** (`TSLAon`, `NVDAon`, `ARKKon`) - Synthetic exposure to US Equities and ETFs.
2.  **Credit-Based Vaults** - Yield generated through institutional credit lending and trade finance.
3.  **Real Estate Vaults** - Exposure to rental yields and property appreciation through tokenized deeds.

### Vault Mechanics

- **Deposit:** User deposits stablecoins (oUSD) or specific RWA tokens into the Smart Vault.
- **Rebalance:** The vault periodically rebalances its underlying strategy to maintain target returns.
- **Withdrawal:** Users can exit their position at any time, with the final value settled through the **Settler's CRE**.
